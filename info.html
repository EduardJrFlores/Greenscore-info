About Greenscore

We're building a sustainable future by making recycling rewarding and accessible for everyone.

Transforming Recycling Through Innovation

Greenscore is a revolutionary digital wallet system that rewards users for their recycling efforts. We believe that environmental responsibility should be incentivized, not just encouraged.

By partnering with collection centers and local businesses, we've created an ecosystem where your sustainable choices translate into real, tangible benefits. From paying utility bills to enjoying discounts at partner stores, your Greenscore points give you the power to live sustainably while saving money.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving change through purpose and innovation

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where environmental responsibility is rewarded, accessible, and beneficial for all Filipinos.

We aim to reduce plastic waste in landfills and oceans by incentivizing proper recycling practices through innovative technology and community partnerships.

Our Vision

A Philippines where zero waste is not just a goal, but a reality—where every citizen actively participates in building a cleaner, greener nation.

We envision Greenscore as the leading platform that transforms waste management into wealth generation, creating lasting positive impact for our environment and communities.
